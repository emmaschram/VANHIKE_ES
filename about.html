ABOUT US

In early 2015, Emma Schram saw a need for Vancouverites and visitors of her beautiful city to be able to enjoy the beauty of the outdoors in its entirety. That day, Vanhike was born.

Vanhike is a specially curated directory of hikes of all difficulties, lengths and unique beauty. Each and every hike has been trailblazed by the Vanhike team to collect as much information about it as possible. We then take that information and input it in our site just for you!

We are a small team of 3 here at Vanhike with a deep love for the outdoors. Join us in encouraging our community to love it as much as we do.

Emma Schram © 2015
